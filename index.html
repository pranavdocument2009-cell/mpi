FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?+
Standard MOQ for recycled LDPE polybags is 500 kg. For custom-printed bags, MOQ varies based on requirements.
Do you offer custom sizes and printing?+
Yes, custom sizes, thicknesses, and flexographic printing up to 6 colors.
What is your delivery timeline?+
Standard: 2-5 working days. Custom: 5-10 working days. Delivery across India.
Are recycled LDPE products food-safe?+
Recycled LDPE is for non-food packaging. For food contact, use our virgin LDPE/LLDPE products.
What thickness for stretch films?+
15-40 microns. Widths 300mm to 1500mm. Power and manual grades available.
Do you provide samples?+
Yes, samples dispatched within 1-2 working days via WhatsApp or inquiry form.
What payment methods accepted?+
NEFT/RTGS, UPI, cheques. Credit terms (15-30 days) for established B2B clients. GST invoices provided.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our products and services.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for polybags? +
Our standard MOQ for recycled LDPE polybags is 500 kg. For custom-printed bags, the MOQ may vary based on print requirements and bag dimensions. Contact us for specific product MOQ details.
Do you offer custom sizes and printing? +
Yes, we specialize in custom manufacturing. We offer custom sizes, thicknesses (gauge), and flexographic printing up to 6 colors. Share your specifications and we'll provide a detailed quote.
What is your delivery timeline? +
Standard products: 2-5 working days. Custom orders: 5-10 working days depending on specifications and order volume. We deliver across Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and all major Indian cities through our logistics network.
Are your recycled LDPE products food-safe? +
Our recycled LDPE products are suitable for non-food industrial packaging. For direct food contact applications, we recommend our virgin LDPE/LLDPE products that meet food-grade safety standards.
What thickness options are available for stretch films? +
We manufacture stretch films in thicknesses ranging from 15 microns to 40 microns. Standard widths include 300mm, 400mm, 500mm, and custom widths up to 1500mm. Available in both power and manual grades.
Do you provide samples before bulk orders? +
Yes, we provide product samples for quality evaluation before placing bulk orders. Sample requests can be made via WhatsApp or our inquiry form. Samples are typically dispatched within 1-2 working days.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS), UPI payments, and cheques. For established B2B clients, we offer credit terms (15-30 days) subject to approval. GST invoices are provided for all orders.
